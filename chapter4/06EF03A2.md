## Problem 2 (Intermediate)
A calibration system measures a normalized sensor error $X$. To report a risk score, the engineer transforms the measurement into $Y=X^2$.
- The random variable $X$ is uniformly distributed on the interval $[1,3]$.
- The PDF of $X$ is $f_X(x)=\frac{1}{2}$ for $1 \le x \le 3$.
- The transformed random variable is $Y=X^2$.

Determine the **probability density function $f_Y(y)$**.
