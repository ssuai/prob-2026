## **[Hard] Topic: Law of Iterated Expectations**

**Problem:** The number of calls $N$ arriving at a center in one hour follows a Poisson distribution with a mean of $10$. The duration of each call $X_i$ is independent and follows an exponential distribution with a mean of $3$ minutes. Find the expected total call duration $T = X_1 + X_2 + \dots + X_N$ in one hour.
