## 1. [Easy] Linear Transformation of a Random Variable

Let $X$ be a random variable following a normal distribution $N(\mu, \sigma^2)$ with mean $\mu = 4$ and variance $\sigma^2 = 9$. Consider a new random variable $Y$ defined by the linear transformation $Y = -3X + 7$. Find the expected value $E[Y]$ and the variance $\text{var}(Y)$.
