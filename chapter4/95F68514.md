# Problem 1

The PMF of the random variable \(X\) is given by:

$$
P(X=0)=\frac{1}{2}, \qquad P(X=1)=\frac{1}{3}, \qquad P(X=3)=\frac{1}{6}.
$$

Define a new random variable $Y = 2X - 1$.

Find the following:

1. The PMF of $Y$
2. $M_Y(s)$

---

Answer : 

(1)

$$
P(Y=-1)=\frac{1}{2}, \qquad P(Y=1)=\frac{1}{3}, \qquad P(Y=5)=\frac{1}{6}.
$$

(2)

$$
M_Y(s) = \frac{1}{2}e^{-s} + \frac{1}{3}e^{s} + \frac{1}{6}e^{5s}.
$$
