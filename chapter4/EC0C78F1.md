## Problem 2: Expected Values and Covariance
Roll a fair die once. Let $X$ be the outcome ($1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6$) and let $Y = X \pmod 2$ (i.e., $Y = 1$ if $X$ is odd, and $Y = 0$ if $X$ is even).

**Questions:**
(a) Compute $E[X]$, $E[Y]$, $E[XY]$, and $\text{Cov}(X, Y)$.

(b) Compute the conditional expectations $E[X | Y = 1]$ and $E[X | Y = 0]$.

(c) Using your answers from parts (a) and (b), verify the Law of Total Expectation: $E[E[X|Y]] = E[X]$.
