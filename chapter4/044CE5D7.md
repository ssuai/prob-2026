Suppose a random variable $X$ follows a normal distribution $\mathcal{N}(\mu, \sigma^2)$. A new random variable is defined by the linear transformation $Y = aX + b$ (where $a \neq 0$). Derive the Moment Generating Function (MGF) of $Y$, denoted as $M_Y(s)$, using $M_X(s)$. Based on the derived MGF, logically prove what distribution the random variable $Y$ follows.
