# Problem 1 — Derived Distribution of a Non-Monotonic Function

**Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Problem Statement

Let $X$ be uniformly distributed on $[-1, 2]$, so $f_X(x) = \tfrac{1}{3}$ for $x \in [-1, 2]$ and $0$ otherwise. Define $Y = X^2$.

### Sub-parts

**(a)** Determine the support of $Y$.

**(b)** Compute the CDF $F_Y(y)$ as a piecewise function.

**(c)** Differentiate to obtain the PDF $f_Y(y)$.

**(d)** Compute $E[Y]$ and $\mathrm{var}(Y)$ using $f_Y(y)$ from part (c).
