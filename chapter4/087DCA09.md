
**Problem 1: Derived Distributions and MGFs**
Let $X$ be a standard normal random variable, $X \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 1)$. Define a new random variable $Y = e^X$.

**Part A:** Using the CDF method, derive the probability density function (PDF), $f_Y(y)$, of $Y$. Be sure to specify the support of $Y$.
**Part B:** The moment generating function (MGF) of $X$ is given by $M_X(s) = e^{s^2/2}$. Use this MGF to find the general formula for the $n$-th moment of $Y$, $E[Y^n]$, for any integer $n \geq 1$.
