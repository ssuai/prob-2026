# Problem 1: App Notification and Study Focus

A student records what happens during a short study session. There are five equally likely situations:

| Situation | Notification Type | Focus Change |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Social media notification | No change |
| 2 | No notification | Focus decreases |
| 3 | No notification | No change |
| 4 | No notification | Focus increases |
| 5 | School-related notification | No change |

Let \(X\) represent the **type of notification**:

\[
X =
\begin{cases}
-1, & \text{social media notification} \\
0, & \text{no notification} \\
1, & \text{school-related notification}
\end{cases}
\]

Let \(Y\) represent the **change in focus**:

\[
Y =
\begin{cases}
-1, & \text{focus decreases} \\
0, & \text{focus stays the same} \\
1, & \text{focus increases}
\end{cases}
\]

## Questions

**(a)** Find the joint pmf \(p_{X,Y}(x,y)\).

**(b)** Find the marginal pmfs \(p_X(x)\) and \(p_Y(y)\) by using marginalization.

**(c)** Calculate \(\operatorname{cov}(X,Y)\).

**(d)** Check whether \(X\) and \(Y\) are independent. Then write a conclusion.
