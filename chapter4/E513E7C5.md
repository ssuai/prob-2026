# Problem 2 (Intermediate) — Derived Distribution & Covariance

## Given

Let $X$ be uniformly distributed on $[0, 1]$, and define

$$U = X, \qquad V = X^2.$$

## Find

1. The PDF $f_V(v)$ of $V$.
2. $E[U]$, $E[V]$, $\text{var}(U)$, and $\text{var}(V)$.
3. The covariance $\text{cov}(U, V)$ and the correlation coefficient $\rho(U, V)$.

## Reflection Question

Are $U$ and $V$ independent? Are they uncorrelated? Explain briefly.

> **Hint:**
> - For part 1, note that $g(x) = x^2$ is strictly monotone on $[0,1]$; apply the monotone-function formula.
> - For part 3, use $\text{cov}(U,V) = E[UV] - E[U]E[V]$ and compute $E[UV] = E[X \cdot X^2] = E[X^3]$.
