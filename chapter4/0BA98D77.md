# Problem 2

Let $X \sim \text{Uniform}(-1,1)$ and let $Y = X^2$.

Find:

1. $F_Y(y)$
2. $f_Y(y)$
3. $E[Y]$ and $\mathrm{Var}(Y)$

---

# Solution


$$
F_Y(y)=
\begin{cases}
0, & y<0 \\
\sqrt{y}, & 0 \le y \le 1 \\
1, & y>1
\end{cases}
\qquad
f_Y(y)=
\begin{cases}
\frac{1}{2\sqrt{y}}, & 0<y<1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

$$
E[Y]=\frac{1}{3}, \qquad \mathrm{Var}(Y)=\frac{4}{45}
$$
