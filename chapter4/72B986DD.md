# Problem 2 (Intermediate)

The number of customers $N$ entering a store in one hour follows a Geometric distribution with parameter $p \in (0,1)$, i.e., $P(N = n) = (1-p)^{n-1}p$ for $n = 1, 2, 3, \dots$. Each customer independently spends an amount $S_i$ that is exponentially distributed with rate $\lambda > 0$. Using the Law of Total Variance, find $\text{Var}\!\left(\sum_{i=1}^{N} S_i\right)$ in terms of $p$ and $\lambda$.
