# Problem 3 (Hard)

Let $X$ and $Y$ be two independent random variables, both uniformly distributed on the interval $[0, 1]$. Define two new random variables as $Z = X + Y$ and $W = X - Y$. 

**Question:** (a) Find the covariance between $Z$ and $W$, $cov(Z, W)$, to determine if they are uncorrelated. 
(b) Determine whether $Z$ and $W$ are independent. Justify your answer.
