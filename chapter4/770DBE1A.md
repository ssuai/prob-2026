# Problem (Intermediate) — Covariance and Correlation of Coin Flips

A biased coin with probability of heads $p = 1/3$ is tossed $n = 12$ times.

Let:
- $X$ = number of heads
- $Y$ = number of tails

**(a)** Find $E[X]$, $E[Y]$, $\text{var}(X)$, and $\text{var}(Y)$.

**(b)** Find $\text{cov}(X, Y)$ and the correlation coefficient $\rho(X, Y)$.

**(c)** Are $X$ and $Y$ independent? Justify your answer.
