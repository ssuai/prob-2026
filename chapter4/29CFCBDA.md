## Problem 3: Moments and Estimation

A random variable $X$ has the Moment Generating Function (MGF):
$$M_X(s) = \frac{1}{1 - 2s}, \quad \text{for } s < 1/2$$

1. Find the mean $E[X]$ and variance $var(X)$ using the MGF.
2. Suppose we observe a related variable $Y = X + W$, where $W$ is a noise term independent of $X$ with $E[W] = 0$. Using the Law of Iterated Expectations, prove that the estimator $\hat{X} = E[X|Y]$ is unbiased.
3. If $E[X|Y] = 0.7Y + 0.3$, and we observe $Y = 5$, what is the best estimate of $X$?
