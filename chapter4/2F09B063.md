## Problem 3 (Hard)
An autonomous vehicle records two normalized delay variables during a route. Let $Y$ be the total delay and let $X$ be the part caused by traffic signal waiting.
- The joint PDF is $f_{X,Y}(x,y)=2$ for $0<x<y<1$ and $0$ otherwise.
- The random variables $X$ and $Y$ are continuous.
- Use the conditional PDF formula $f_{X|Y}(x|y)=\frac{f_{X,Y}(x,y)}{f_Y(y)}$.

Calculate the **conditional variance $\operatorname{var}(X|Y=y)$** for $0<y<1$.
