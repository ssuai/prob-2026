# Problem 3 (Hard)

Let $X$ and $Y$ be independent random variables, where $X \sim \text{Exponential}(\lambda)$ and $Y \sim \text{Exponential}(\mu)$ with $\lambda \neq \mu$. Define $W = X + Y$. Using moment generating functions, derive the MGF of $W$, perform a partial fraction decomposition, and from this obtain the closed-form PDF $f_W(w)$ for $w > 0$.
