# Problem

Let the joint PDF of $X$ and $Y$ be

$$
f_{X,Y}(x,y)=
\begin{cases}
2, & 0< y < x <1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

Find the following:

1. $f_Y(y)$
2. $f_{X|Y}(x|y)$
3. $E[X|Y=y]$, $\mathrm{Var}(X|Y=y)$
4. $E[X]$, $E[Y]$
5. $\mathrm{cov}(X,Y)$

---


# Solution

## 1. Marginal PDF of \(Y\)

$$
f_Y(y)=\int_y^1 2\,dx = 2(1-y), \qquad 0<y<1
$$


## 2. Conditional PDF

$$
f_{X|Y}(x|y)=
\begin{cases}
\dfrac{1}{1-y}, & y<x<1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

## 3. Conditional Expectation / Variance

$$
E[X|Y=y]=\frac{y+1}{2}
$$

$$
\mathrm{Var}(X|Y=y)=\frac{(1-y)^2}{12}
$$

## 4. \(E[X]\), \(E[Y]\)

$$
E[Y]
=
\int_0^1 y\cdot 2(1-y)\,dy
=
2\int_0^1 (y-y^2)\,dy
=
2\left(\frac12-\frac13\right)
=
\frac13
$$

$$
E[X]
=
E[E[X|Y]]
=
E\left[\frac{Y+1}{2}\right]
=
\frac12(E[Y]+1)
=
\frac12\left(\frac13+1\right)
=
\frac23
$$

## 5. Covariance and Correlation

$$
\mathrm{cov}(X,Y)
=
E[XY]-E[X]E[Y]
=
\frac14-\frac23\cdot\frac13
=
\frac14-\frac29
=
\frac1{36}
$$
