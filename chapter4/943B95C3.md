## Problem 3: Conditional Expectation and MGF Trick

Let N be a Poisson random variable with parameter lambda.

Suppose that each of the N items is independently colored red with probability p and blue with probability 1 - p.

Let R be the number of red items.

### Questions

1. Find E[R | N].
2. Find E[R].
3. Find the moment generating function M_R(t).
4. Identify the distribution of R.
5. Find Cov(R, N - R).
