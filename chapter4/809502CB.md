## Problem 2: Variance of Sums and Covariance

Consider two random variables $X$ and $Y$. We are given the following information:
* $var(X) = 4$
* $var(Y) = 9$
* $var(2X - Y) = 21$

1. Calculate the covariance $cov(X, Y)$.
2. Calculate the correlation coefficient $\rho(X, Y)$.
3. Determine $var(X + 3Y)$.
