An investor has constructed a portfolio with two independent assets, A and B. Let the daily return of asset A be the random variable X, and the daily return of asset B be the random variable Y. The moment generating functions (MGF) of each variable are given as M_X(s) = e^{2s^2 + 3s} and M_Y(s) = e^{s^2 - s}. When the total return of the portfolio is defined as W = 2X - Y, what is the variance of the total return, var(W)?
