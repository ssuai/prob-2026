# Problem 2 (Intermediate)

Let $X$ be a continuous random variable that is uniformly distributed over the interval $[1, e]$ (where $e$ is Euler's number, approximately 2.718).
A new random variable $Y$ is defined by the strictly monotonic function:
$Y = \ln(X)$

Find the Probability Density Function (PDF) of $Y$, $f_Y(y)$. You may use either the CDF differentiation method or the direct formula for monotonic functions. Specify the valid range for $y$.
