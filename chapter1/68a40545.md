## 🔴 3️⃣ Hard Problem

There are 10 balls:
- 4 red balls
- 6 blue balls


From these, 5 balls are drawn **without regard to order**.

1. Find the probability that **exactly 2** of the selected balls are red.

2. Given that at least one red ball was selected, find the probability that exactly 2 are red.

3. Define the following events:
- A: exactly 2 red balls
- B: at least 1 red ball

👉 **Are A and B independent?** Explain.

---

**Answers:**

**1: 10/21**

**2: 20/41**

**3: Not independent**
