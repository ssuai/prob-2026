# Problem (Easy)

A box contains 3 red balls and 2 blue balls. Two balls are drawn sequentially without replacement.

1. What is the probability that the second ball is red given that the first ball was blue?
2. Are the events “first ball is blue” and “second ball is red” independent? Justify your answer mathematically.

## Assumptions / Given
- All balls are equally likely to be drawn.
- No replacement between draws.
