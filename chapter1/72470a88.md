## Problem 3 (Hard)
A specialized research team consists of 12 scientists: 4 Physicists, 4 Chemists, and 4 Biologists. The department needs to form a structured project unit through the following process: 

- first, a 6-member committee must be selected such that it contains exactly 2 scientists from each of the three disciplines. 
- Then, these 6 selected members must be partitioned into three distinct labeled working groups (Group A, Group B, and Group C), with each group consisting of exactly 2 members.

Calculate **the total number of distinct ways to complete this entire process**.
