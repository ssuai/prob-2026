# Problem 2 (Intermediate)

An electronics company manufactures smartphones. Overall, 70% of the smartphones are produced in 'Factory A' (Event A1), and the remaining 30% are produced in 'Factory B' (Event A2).

The quality inspection records show the following:
* The probability that a smartphone produced in Factory A is defective (Event B) is 10%.
* The probability that a smartphone produced in Factory B is defective (Event B) is 40%.

One day, a customer purchased a smartphone and found it to be **defective (B)**. Given this information, use **Bayes' rule** to exactly calculate the posterior probability that this defective smartphone was produced in **Factory B (A2)**.
