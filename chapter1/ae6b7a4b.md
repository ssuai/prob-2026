# Problem 2 (Intermediate)

A bag contains 4 white balls, 3 black balls, and 2 green balls.

Two balls are drawn without replacement.

Let:
- A = event that the first ball drawn is white
- B = event that the second ball drawn is black

1. Find P(A), P(B), and P(A ∩ B).
2. Determine whether A and B are independent.
