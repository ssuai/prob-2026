# Problem 3: The Systems Lab Network Outage

**Problem Statement:**
You are configuring a local network for a computer systems lab that has 5 active workstations ($n=5$). The lab's main router can comfortably handle up to 3 simultaneous heavy data transfers. If 4 or 5 workstations attempt a heavy transfer at the exact same time, the router experiences an outage.

The lab operates in two distinct states throughout the day, which form a partition of the sample space:
1. **Algorithm Study Mode ($A_1$):** This occurs 60% of the time. During this mode, each workstation independently initiates a heavy data transfer with a probability of 0.1.
2. **Multiplayer Gaming Mode ($A_2$):** This occurs 40% of the time. During this mode, each workstation independently initiates a heavy data transfer with a probability of 0.8.

**Question:**
Let $B$ be the event that the router experiences an outage. What is the overall probability $P(B)$ at any given randomly selected moment?
