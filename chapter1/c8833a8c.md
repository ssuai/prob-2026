## 📌 Intermediate Problem

A standard deck of 52 playing cards (without jokers) is well shuffled. A player draws one card.

Suppose the first card drawn is a heart (\(\heartsuit\)). The first card is then removed, and a second card is drawn.

Define the following events:

- \( A \): the second card is a number card \( \{2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9,10\} \)  
- \( B \): the first card is a heart (\(\heartsuit\))

Find the conditional probability \( P(A \mid B) \).
