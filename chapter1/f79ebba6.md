### Intermediate (Level: Medium) - Total Probability Theorem

Problem:

There are two boxes, $A$ and $B$.

Box $A$ contains 4 red balls and 2 black balls.

Box $B$ contains 3 red balls and 5 black balls.

A box is selected at random (with a probability of $1/2$ for each box),
and one ball is drawn from the selected box.
Calculate the probability that the drawn ball is red.
