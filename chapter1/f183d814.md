# Problem 1 (Easy)

A fair coin is tossed three times. Let the sample space be

\[
\Omega = \{HHH, HHT, HTH, HTT, THH, THT, TTH, TTT\},
\]

where each outcome is equally likely. Define the following three events:

- \(A_1\): the first toss is Heads  
- \(A_2\): the second toss is Heads  
- \(A_3\): the third toss is Heads  

Answer the following questions.

## (a)

Find \(P(A_1)\), \(P(A_2)\), and \(P(A_3)\).

## (b)

Find \(P(A_1 \cap A_2)\), \(P(A_1 \cap A_3)\), and \(P(A_2 \cap A_3)\).

## (c)

Find \(P(A_1 \cap A_2 \cap A_3)\). Then determine whether the three events \(A_1, A_2, A_3\) are mutually independent.

## (d)

Using your result from part (c), prove that

\[
P(A_3 \mid A_1 \cap A_2) = P(A_3).
\]

Briefly explain what this means in words.
