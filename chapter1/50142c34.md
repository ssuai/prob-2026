# Exercise 1: Conditional Probability and Independence

An experiment involves rolling a fair 4-sided die twice. Let the sample space $\Omega$ be the set of all 16 equally likely outcomes. Define the following two events:
- **Event A**: The maximum of the two rolls is 2.
- **Event B**: The minimum of the two rolls is 2.

**Questions:**
1. List all the outcomes belonging to Event $A$ and Event $B$.
2. Calculate the conditional probability $P(A|B)$.
3. Determine whether events $A$ and $B$ are independent. Justify your answer using the mathematical definition of independence.
