# Problem 1: The 4-Sided Dice Roll

**Problem Statement:**
Suppose you conduct an experiment where you roll a fair 4-sided die two times. 
Let event $A$ be the case where the sum of the two rolls is exactly 6. 
Let event $B$ be the case where the first roll is a 2.

**Questions:**
1. determine the total number of possible outcomes in the sample space $\Omega$.
2. Find the conditional probability $P(A|B)$.
