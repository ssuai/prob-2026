## Problem 1 (Easy)

A museum stores its special exhibition posters in three cabinets: Cabinet A, Cabinet B, and Cabinet C. Exactly one poster is selected at random for inspection.

The storage system is organized as follows:

- 50% of the posters are in Cabinet A, 30% are in Cabinet B, and 20% are in Cabinet C.
- In Cabinet A, 8% of the posters have a printing defect.
- In Cabinet B, 15% of the posters have a printing defect.
- In Cabinet C, 25% of the posters have a printing defect.

Let $D$ be the event that the selected poster has a printing defect.

**Task:** Given that the selected poster has a printing defect, determine the probability that it came from Cabinet C.
