# 1. easy

## 
A factory produces products.
60% of the products are produced by machine A, and 40% by machine B.
The defect rate of machine A is 2%, and the defect rate of machine B is 5%
(1) Find the probability that a randomly chosen product is defective. (Total Probability)
(2) Find the probability that the product was produced by machine A, given that it is defective. (Bayes' Theorem)
(3) Determine if "Being produced by machine A" and "Defect status" are independent. (Independence).
