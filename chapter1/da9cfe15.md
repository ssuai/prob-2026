**Problem**  
Let \( f(x) \) be a function defined on the set of natural numbers such that  
\( f(x) \) is the units digit of \( 3^x \).

A box contains 10 cards labeled with the numbers from 1 to 10. 
One card is drawn three times **with replacement**, and the numbers drawn are denoted by \( a, b, c \) in order.

Find the probability that  
\[
(f(a) - 7)(f(b)f(c) - 9) = 0.
\]
