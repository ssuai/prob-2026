Level 3: Hard (Total Probability & Combinatorics)
Topic: Partitioning and Sampling Without Replacement

You have two identical-looking bags, Bag A and Bag B.
 - Bag A contains 6 Red balls and 4 Blue balls (10 total).
 - Bag B contains 3 Red balls and 7 Blue balls (10 total).

An experiment is performed in two steps:
 Step 1: You flip a fair coin to choose a bag.
 Step 2: From the chosen bag, you pick 3 balls at once (without replacement).

Question: If the 3 balls you picked are all Red, what is the probability that they came from Bag A?
