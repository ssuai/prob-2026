## Problem 1 (Easy)

In a specific Pokémon region, there are **15 distinct species** available for capture.  
A trainer intends to select a subset of **3 unique species** to form a starting team.

- Number of Water-type species: \( |W| = 6 \)
- Number of Flying-type species: \( |F| = 4 \)
- Number of species that are both Water and Flying: \( |W \cap F| = 2 \)

Calculate the **total number of distinct 3-species teams**, and determine the **total number of species that are either Water-type or Flying-type using**.
