# Problem (Intermediate — Total Probability & Bayes’ Theorem)

A factory has two machines, A and B.

- Machine A produces 60% of the items and has a defect rate of 3%.
- Machine B produces 40% of the items and has a defect rate of 6%.

1. If an item is selected at random, what is the probability that it is defective?
2. Given that a randomly selected item is defective, what is the probability it was produced by Machine B?
3. Suppose you inspect two randomly selected items independently. What is the probability that at least one of them is defective?

## Assumptions / Given
- Each item is produced independently.
- Use the law of total probability where needed.
