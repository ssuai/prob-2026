Level 2: Intermediate (Conditional Probability & Bayes' Rule)
Topic: Total Probability Theorem

In a city, 40% of the days are Cloudy and 60% are Sunny.
 On a Cloudy day, the probability of rain is 80%.
 On a Sunny day, the probability of rain is only 10%.

Question: If it is raining right now, what is the probability that today is a Cloudy day?
(Hint: Use the Total Probability Theorem to find the overall chance of rain first, then apply Bayes' Rule.)
