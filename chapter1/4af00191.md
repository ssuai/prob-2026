Problem 1 (Easy)

In a class of 30 students:

•	18 students like Mathematics (M)
•	12 students like Statistics (S)
•	5 students like both Mathematics and Statistics

a)	What is the probability that a randomly selected student likes Mathematics?
b)	What is the probability that a student likes Mathematics or Statistics?
c)	What is the probability that a student likes Mathematics given that they like Statistics?
