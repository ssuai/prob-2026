A factory uses three machines (M₁, M₂, M₃) to produce bolts.
M₁ produces 50% of the bolts and has a 2% defect rate.
M₂ produces 30% of the bolts and has a 5% defect rate.
M₃ produces 20% of the bolts and has a 10% defect rate.
If a bolt is selected at random and found to be defective, what is the probability that it was produced by machine M₃?
