## 🟡 2️⃣ Medium Problem

A factory produces products using two machines:

- Machine A: produces **70%** of the total, defect rate = **0.1**
- Machine B: produces **30%** of the total, defect rate = **0.2**

A randomly selected product is found to be **defective**.

👉 Find the probability that this product was produced by **Machine A**.

---

**Answer: 7/13**
