# Hard Problem – Conditional + Total Probability

A student can solve a problem using one of two methods:

- Method A is used with probability 0.6 and succeeds with probability 0.7.
- Method B is used with probability 0.4 and succeeds with probability 0.9.

Given that the student successfully solved the problem, what is the probability they used Method A?

Also, compute the total probability of success.
