# Hard Problem

A class has 5 students: 3 males and 2 females. Two students are randomly selected without replacement.

1. Define the sample space using set notation.
2. What is the probability that both selected students are male?
3. What is the probability that the second student is female given that the first student selected is male? (conditional probability)
4. Are the events "first student is male" and "second student is female" independent? Justify your answer.
5. Using counting techniques, calculate the total number of possible selections and verify your probabilities.
