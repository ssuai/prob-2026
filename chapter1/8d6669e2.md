Topic: Sample Space and Conditional Probability

Consider an experiment where you roll a fair 4-sided die (with faces numbered 1, 2, 3, 4) twice independently.
Let X be the result of the first roll and Y be the result of the second roll.

1. Define the sample space Ω for this experiment and find the total number of possible outcomes.
2. What is the conditional probability that the sum of the two rolls is exactly 6, given that the first roll resulted in an even number (X∈{2,4})?
