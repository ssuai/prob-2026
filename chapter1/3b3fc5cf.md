### [Intermediate]

A cloud service provider operates a cluster of servers where the probability of a single server experiencing a connection timeout during a high-traffic period is **0.1**. 

Assuming that the connection status of each server is **independent** of the others, calculate the probability that **exactly 2** servers will experience timeouts out of a random sample of **5 servers**. 

Provide the final result by applying the binomial probability formula.
