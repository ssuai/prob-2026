## Problem: Central Limit Theorem Approximation

A delivery company ships \(100\) packages in one truck. The weights of the packages are independent and identically distributed as

\[
X_i\sim \operatorname{Uniform}(10,40),
\]

where \(X_i\) is the weight, in kilograms, of the \(i\)-th package.

Let

\[
S_{100}=X_1+X_2+\cdots+X_{100}.
\]

Answer the following questions.

### Questions

1. Find \(E[X_i]\) and \(\operatorname{var}(X_i)\).
2. Find \(E[S_{100}]\) and \(\operatorname{var}(S_{100})\).
3. Standardize \(S_{100}\) using

\[
Z=\frac{S_{100}-E[S_{100}]}{\sqrt{\operatorname{var}(S_{100})}}.
\]

---

### Notes

For \(X\sim \operatorname{Uniform}(a,b)\),

\[
E[X]=\frac{a+b}{2},
\]

\[
\operatorname{var}(X)=\frac{(b-a)^2}{12}.
\]

You may use the standard normal CDF notation \(\Phi(z)\).
