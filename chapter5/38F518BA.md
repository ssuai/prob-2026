### **\[Easy] Topic: Markov \& Chebyshev Inequalities**

**Problem:**
The number of requests per second arriving at a web server is a non-negative random variable $X$ with a mean (expected value) of $E\[X] = 50$ and a variance of $var(X) = 25$.

1. Use **Markov's Inequality** to find an upper bound on the probability that the number of requests per second is at least 150.
2. Use **Chebyshev's Inequality** to find an upper bound on the probability that the number of requests per second is either less than or equal to 30, or greater than or equal to 70.
