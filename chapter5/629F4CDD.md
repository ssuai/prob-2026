# Easy Problem: Probability Inequalities

A non-negative random variable \(X\) represents the number of daily support tickets at a tech company. You are told that:

\[
E[X] = 10
\]

\[
\mathrm{Var}(X) = 4
\]

## Questions

1. Use Markov’s inequality to find an upper bound for:

\[
P(X \ge 20)
\]

2. Use Chebyshev’s inequality to find an upper bound for:

\[
P(|X - 10| \ge 6)
\]
