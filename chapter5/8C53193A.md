# Problem 2 — Intermediate

## Topic
Chebyshev Inequality

## Problem
Let \(X\) be a random variable representing the score of a randomly selected student on a statistics quiz. Suppose that

\[
E[X] = 75, \qquad Var(X) = 36.
\]

Using Chebyshev inequality, answer the following questions.

1. Find an upper bound for

\[
P(|X - 75| \ge 12).
\]

2. Find a lower bound for the probability that the score is between 63 and 87.

3. Find an upper bound for the probability that the score differs from the mean by at least 18 points.

Show all steps clearly.
