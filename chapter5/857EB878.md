# Exercise 3: Application of the Central Limit Theorem (CLT)
**Level:** Hard

## Problem
An airplane is loaded with 100 independent packages. The weight of each package $X_i$ follows a uniform distribution between 5 kg and 45 kg ($X_i \sim U[5, 45]$). The airplane has a strict structural weight limit of 2,700 kg for these packages.

Using the **Central Limit Theorem**, calculate the approximate probability that the total weight of the 100 packages exceeds the airplane's limit.

*Note: For a uniform distribution $U[a, b]$, the mean is $\frac{a+b}{2}$ and the variance is $\frac{(b-a)^2}{12}$. Provide your answer in terms of the standard normal CDF $\Phi(z)$.*
