Hard

The weights of 64 packages are independent and identically distributed random variables.
Each package weight is uniformly distributed on [2,10] kilograms.

Let
S=X1+X2+⋯+X64.

Use the Central Limit Theorem (CLT) to approximate
P(S>420).

You may use the following facts for a uniform random variable on [a,b]:

E[X]=a+b/2, Var(X)=(b−a)**2/12.
