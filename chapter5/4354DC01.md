3.md (Hard)

A factory produces electronic devices. The lifetime $X_i$ (in hours) of each device is independent and identically distributed (i.i.d.) with the following parameters:Mean: $E[X_i] = 100$Variance: $Var(X_i) = 225$Suppose 81 devices are selected independently. Let $S_{81}$ be the sum of their lifetimes:$$S_{81} = X_1 + X_2 + \dots + X_{81}$$QuestionUsing the Central Limit Theorem (CLT), approximate the following probability:$$P(S_{81} \geq 8500)$$(Note: Use the approximation $\Phi(1.48) \approx 0.9306$ if necessary.)
