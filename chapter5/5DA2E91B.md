# Problem 2 (Intermediate)

A logistics company is loading 100 identical boxes onto a cargo truck. The weight of each box is independent and identically distributed, with a mean of 20 kg and a variance of 25 kg^2. 

**Question:** Let $S_{100}$ be the total weight of the 100 boxes. Using the Central Limit Theorem, approximate the probability that the total weight exceeds 2100 kg. (Express your final answer in terms of the standard normal cumulative distribution function, $\Phi(z)$).
