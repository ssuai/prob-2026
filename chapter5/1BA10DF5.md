# Problem 2

Suppose

$$ X_1,\ldots,X_{36} $$

are i.i.d. random variables with

$$ E[X_i]=50, \qquad \mathrm{Var}(X_i)=16. $$

For the sample mean 
$$ M_n = \frac{X_1 + \cdots + X_n}{n} $$
find an upper bound for

$$ P(|M_{36}-50|\ge 1). $$

---

## Solution


$$
\frac49
$$
