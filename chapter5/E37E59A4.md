# Hard Problem

A delivery company loads $120$ packages onto a truck. The package weights are independent and uniformly distributed between $2$ kg and $8$ kg. Let $X_i$ be the weight of the $i$-th package. Thus:

$$X_i \sim \operatorname{Uniform}[2,8]$$

Use the Central Limit Theorem to approximate:

$$P(X_1 + X_2 + \cdots + X_{120} > 630)$$

Your solution should include:

1. The mean and variance of one package weight.
2. The mean and variance of the total weight.
3. The standardized $Z$-value.
4. The final probability approximation using the standard normal distribution.
