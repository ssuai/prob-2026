🛑 Hardness Level: Low (Markov Inequality)
Problem 1: Food Delivery LogisticsA popular food delivery application analyzes its data and finds that the average delivery time for a local order is exactly $24\text{ minutes}$.
 Because traffic conditions fluctuate wildly, the exact probability distribution of delivery times is unknown. However, delivery times are guaranteed to be non-negative.
 Using the Markov Inequality:
Question: Find the maximum possible probability that a randomly selected delivery will take $1\text{ hour}$ ($60\text{ minutes}$) or longer.
