## Problem 3 (Hard)
A reliability lab tests many small components. For each component, let $X_i$ be a normalized lifetime score between $0$ and $1$, and let $S_{144}=X_1+\cdots+X_{144}$.
- The random variables $X_1,\ldots,X_{144}$ are **i.i.d.**
- Each $X_i$ has PDF $f_X(x)=2x$ for $0<x<1$ and $0$ otherwise.
- Use the **central limit theorem** to approximate the distribution of $S_{144}$.
- You may use $\Phi(1.41)=0.9207$ and $\Phi(2.12)=0.9830$.

Determine the approximate value of **$P(90<S_{144}<100)$**.
