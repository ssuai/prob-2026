# Problem 1 (Easy)

Let $X$ be a nonnegative random variable representing the waiting time, in minutes, for a small repair request. Suppose

$$
E[X]=6.
$$

Task: Use Markov's inequality to find an upper bound for $P(X\ge 15)$.

---

# Solution

Since $X\ge 0$, Markov's inequality gives

$$
P(X\ge a)\le \frac{E[X]}{a}.
$$

With $a=15$,

$$
P(X\ge 15)\le \frac{6}{15}=\frac{2}{5}.
$$

Therefore,

$$
P(X\ge 15)\le 0.4.
$$
