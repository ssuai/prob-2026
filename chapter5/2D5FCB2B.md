# Problem 3. Central Limit Theorem

A delivery company handles 75 packages in one day.

The weight of each package is independent and uniformly distributed between 4 kg and 16 kg.

Let $X_i$ be the weight of the $i$-th package.

Using the Central Limit Theorem, approximate

$$
P(X_1 + X_2 + \cdots + X_{75} > 780).
$$

You may use the following standard normal CDF values:

$$
\Phi(0.5) = 0.6915,\quad
\Phi(1.0) = 0.8413,\quad
\Phi(1.5) = 0.9332,\quad
\Phi(2.0) = 0.9772.
$$

Show your work clearly.
