# Problem 2 (Intermediate)

The daily revenue X_i (in units of 10,000 KRW) of a cafe is an i.i.d. random variable
with E[X_i] = 80 and var(X_i) = 225.

(a) Using the Chebyshev inequality, find an upper bound for P(|X_i - 80| ≥ 45).

(b) Let M_100 = (X_1 + ... + X_100) / 100 be the sample mean over 100 days.
    Find an upper bound for P(|M_100 - 80| ≥ 45) and compare it with (a).

(c) Find the minimum n such that P(|M_n - 80| ≥ 45) ≤ 0.01.

(d) Interpret the result of (c) in terms of the Weak Law of Large Numbers.
