# Problem 3 — Hard

## Topic
Central Limit Theorem

## Problem
A delivery company loads \(80\) packages onto a truck. Assume that the package weights are independent and identically distributed random variables. Each package weight \(X_i\) is uniformly distributed between \(2\) kg and \(12\) kg:

\[
X_i \sim Uniform[2,12].
\]

Let

\[
S_{80} = X_1 + X_2 + \cdots + X_{80}
\]

be the total weight of the packages.

Using the Central Limit Theorem, approximate the probability

\[
P(S_{80} > 600).
\]

Your solution should include:

1. The mean and variance of one package weight.
2. The mean and variance of \(S_{80}\).
3. The standardization step using a standard normal random variable.
4. The final approximate probability.
