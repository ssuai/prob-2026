Hard Problem — Central Limit Theorem

The weights of rice bags produced by a company are normally distributed with mean 50 kg and standard deviation 4 kg.

A random sample of 64 rice bags is selected.

Using the Central Limit Theorem, calculate the probability that the sample mean weight is greater than 49 kg.

Show all calculations, including:
- the sampling distribution,
- the standard error,
- the z-score,
- and the final probability.
