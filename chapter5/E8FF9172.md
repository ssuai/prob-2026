Question 3. Difficulty \[High]

The weight of a single metal part produced in a factory follows an arbitrary distribution with a mean of 15g and a variance of 16g^2. If 100 of these parts are randomly selected and packed into a box, what is the approximate probability that the total weight of the parts is 1,420g or less? Express your answer using the standard normal cumulative distribution function Φ(z) by applying the Central Limit Theorem.
