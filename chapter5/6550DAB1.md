## Problem: Markov and Chebyshev Inequalities

Let \(X\) be a random variable representing the daily waiting time, in minutes, at a service desk.

Assume that

\[
X \ge 0,\qquad E[X]=12,\qquad \operatorname{var}(X)=16.
\]

Answer the following questions.

### Questions

1. Using Markov's inequality, find an upper bound for

\[
P(X\ge 30).
\]

2. Using Chebyshev's inequality, find an upper bound for

\[
P(|X-12|\ge 8).
\]

3. Using Chebyshev's inequality, find a lower bound for

\[
P(4<X<20).
\]

---

### Notes

You may use

\[
P(X\ge a)\le \frac{E[X]}{a}, \qquad a>0,
\]

and

\[
P(|X-\mu|\ge c)\le \frac{\sigma^2}{c^2}, \qquad c>0.
\]
