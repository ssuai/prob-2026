### **\[Intermediate] Topic: Central Limit Theorem**

**Problem:**
The lifespan of a battery produced in a factory follows an unknown distribution with a mean of 40 hours and a standard deviation of 5 hours. If a random sample of 100 batteries is selected, use the **Central Limit Theorem (CLT)** to find the probability that the sample mean lifespan ($\\bar{X}$) is between 39 and 41 hours. (Assume $\\Phi(2) \\approx 0.9772$ from the standard normal distribution table.)
