# Problem 3

Suppose

$$ X_1, X_2, \ldots, X_n $$

are i.i.d. random variables with

$$ E[X_i] = 10 $$

$$ \mathrm{Var}(X_i) = 4 $$

Let the sample mean be

$$ M_n = \frac{X_1 + \cdots + X_n}{n} $$

Find the smallest value of $n$ such that

$$ P(|M_n - 10| \ge 1) \le 0.05 $$

## Solution

$$ 80 $$
