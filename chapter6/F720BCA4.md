\# Problem 2 (Intermediate Level)

\## Topic: Poisson Merging + Poisson PMF



\### Problem Statement

A web server receives two distinct types of requests:

\- \*\*Type 1 requests\*\* arrive according to a Poisson process with a rate of $\\lambda\_1 = 3$ per minute.

\- \*\*Type 2 requests\*\* arrive independently according to a Poisson process with a rate of $\\lambda\_2 = 5$ per minute.



We monitor the merged stream of all incoming requests.



\### Question

What is the probability that exactly 2 requests (of any type) arrive at the server during a specific 30-second interval?
