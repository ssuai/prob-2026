# Problem 3 (Hard)

A customer service hotline receives calls according to a Poisson process with a rate of $\lambda = 4$ calls per minute. Let $Y_{64}$ be the waiting time (in minutes) until the 64th call arrives. 

**Question:**
(a) Identify the exact probability distribution of $Y_{64}$, and calculate its expected value and variance.
(b) Using the Central Limit Theorem, approximate the probability that the 64th call arrives after more than 18 minutes. (Express your final answer using the standard normal cumulative distribution function, $\Phi(z)$).
