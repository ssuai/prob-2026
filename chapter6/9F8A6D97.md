## Topic: Geometric Distribution and Memorylessness of the Bernoulli Process

---

## Problem Statement

A factory quality-control line inspects items one at a time. Each item is
defective independently with probability $p = 0.2$.

Let $T$ be the number of items inspected **up to and including** the first
defective item.

**(a)** Write down the PMF of $T$ and compute $E[T]$ and $\text{Var}(T)$.

**(b)** The inspector has already checked 4 items without finding a defect.
Given this information, what is the probability that **at least 3 more**
items must be inspected before finding the first defect?
