An online shopping mall receives an average of 10 orders per minute (λ = 10), following a Poisson process. According to historical data, 20% of all orders are 'next-day rocket delivery' orders, and the remaining 80% are 'regular delivery' orders. The delivery type for each order is determined independently. Let the random variable Y₅ be the time that elapses from the moment a logistics center worker turns on the system until exactly the 5th 'next-day rocket delivery' order arrives. What is the variance of this waiting time Y₅? (Unit: minute²)
