# 2. Intermediate Problem

A machine checks whether a product is defective. Each product is defective with probability $p=0.08$, independently of other products.

Let $T$ be the number of products checked up to and including the first defective product.

1. What distribution does $T$ follow, and find $P(T=5)$.
2. Find $P(T>10)$, $E[T]$, and $\operatorname{Var}(T)$.
3. Let $Y_3$ be the product number on which the third defective product appears. Write the formula for $P(Y_3=t)$, where $t=3,4,5,\ldots$, and find $E[Y_3]$.
