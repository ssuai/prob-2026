# Bernoulli Process — Hard Problem

## Problem 3

In a basketball game, player Alice commits a foul in each minute with probability **p = 0.3**, independently across minutes. She fouls out after her **5th foul** and will play at most **40 minutes**.

Her playing time is defined as:
$$Z = \min\{Y_5, 40\}$$

where $Y_5$ is the time of the 5th foul.

**(a)** What is the PMF of $Y_5$? State its name and write the full expression.

**(b)** Write the **full PMF of Z**, carefully handling all cases.

**(c)** What is the probability that Alice **fouls out before the end** of the game?

**(d)** What is the probability that Alice **plays the full 40 minutes**?

**(e)** Given that Alice has already committed **3 fouls in the first 10 minutes**, what is the probability that she fouls out **before the game ends**?
