## Problem 2
A spam filter checks emails arriving at rate $p = 0.6$ per slot (Bernoulli process). Each email is independently classified as spam with probability $q = 0.4$, and forwarded to folder A with probability $0.5$ or folder B with probability $0.5$ if not spam.

**Questions:**

(a) Find the parameters of the spam process and the non-spam process after splitting.

(b) Let $Y_3$ be the slot number of the 3rd email to reach folder A. Find $E[Y_3]$ and $\text{Var}(Y_3)$.

(c) Folders A and B later merge their inboxes. What is the parameter of the merged process? Find the expected waiting time until the first merged arrival.
