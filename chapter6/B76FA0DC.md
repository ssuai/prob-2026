# Problem: Emergency Drone Inspection

A city uses an automated drone system to inspect power lines after a storm. Damage reports arrive at the control center according to a Poisson process with rate

\[
\lambda = 0.4
\]

reports per hour.

The drone team begins monitoring at 8:00 AM. The team must monitor for at least 3 hours. If at least 4 damage reports have arrived by the end of the 3-hour monitoring period, the team stops monitoring and immediately begins dispatching drones. If fewer than 4 reports have arrived by then, the team continues monitoring until the 4th damage report arrives, and then stops.

Let \(S\) be the total amount of time, in hours after 8:00 AM, that the team spends monitoring.

**(a)** Find the probability that the team monitors for more than 3 hours.

**(b)** Find the probability that exactly 4 reports have arrived by the time the team stops monitoring.

**(c)** Find the expected stopping time \(E[S]\).
