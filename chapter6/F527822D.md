# Problem 3 (Hard)

Three identical light bulbs are turned on simultaneously in a chandelier. Each bulb has an independent exponential lifetime with a parameter of $\lambda$. Let $T_{total}$ be the total time from when they are turned on until the *last* bulb burns out. 

Show your step-by-step reasoning to express the expected total time, $\mathbb{E}[T_{total}]$, in terms of $\lambda$.
