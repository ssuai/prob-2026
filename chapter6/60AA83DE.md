# Question 2 (Intermediate): Merging Poisson Processes & Exponential Interarrival Time

An AI service processes two independent streams of requests. Text processing requests arrive as a Poisson process with rate $\lambda_1 = 3$ requests per second. Image processing requests arrive as an independent Poisson process with rate $\lambda_2 = 2$ requests per second.
Consider the merged process of all incoming requests. Let $T$ be the time (in seconds) until the very first request (either text or image) arrives.
1. What is the distribution of $T$? (State the distribution name and its parameter).
2. Calculate the probability that no requests arrive in the first 0.5 seconds, i.e., $P(T > 0.5)$.
