\## \[Intermediate] Topic: Maximum Likelihood (ML) Estimation of an Exponential Distribution



The waiting time $X$ for requests on a web server follows an exponential distribution with parameter $\\theta$. Its Probability Density Function (PDF) is given by:

$$f\_X(x; \\theta) = \\theta e^{- \\theta x} \\quad (x \\ge 0, \\; \\theta > 0)$$



Suppose you independently collect three samples of waiting times: $X\_1 = 1$, $X\_2 = 2$, and $X\_3 = 3$. Find the \*\*Maximum Likelihood (ML) estimate\*\* ($\\hat{\\theta}\_{ML}$) for the parameter $\\theta$.
