# Poisson Process — Easy Problem

## Problem 1

Customers arrive at a coffee shop according to a Poisson process with a rate of **λ = 3 customers per hour**.

**(a)** What is the probability that **exactly 3 customers** arrive in a 1-hour period?

**(b)** What is the probability that **no customers** arrive in a 30-minute period?

**(c)** What is the **expected number** of customers in a 2-hour period?
