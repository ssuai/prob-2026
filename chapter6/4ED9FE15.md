# Easy Problem: Bernoulli Process

A student checks their phone once every 10 minutes.  
Each time they check, there is a probability of 0.3 that they receive at least one message.  
Assume each check is independent.

1. What is the probability that the student receives messages exactly 2 times in the first 5 checks?
2. What is the probability that the first message is received on the 4th check?
3. What is the expected number of checks until the first message is received?
