## Problem 2 (Intermediate)

A busy toll plaza receives vehicles according to a Poisson process at a constant rate of 120 vehicles per hour. Upon arriving at the plaza, each vehicle has an independent $25\%$ probability of being classified as a heavy commercial truck, while the remaining $75\%$ are classified as passenger cars. An automated sensor system is installed to specifically monitor and record the arrivals of the heavy commercial trucks. Determine the probability that the waiting time between the arrival of the first heavy commercial truck and the arrival of the second heavy commercial truck observed by the sensor system is strictly greater than 5 minutes but less than 15 minutes.
