Customers arrive at a dual-service bank according to a Poisson process with a rate of $lambda = 3 customers per minute. Upon arrival, each customer independently decides to use an Automated Teller Machine (ATM) with probability p = 1/3 or consult a human teller with probability 1-p = 2/3. Let Y_4 denote the continuous time (in minutes) until the 4th customer who chooses to use the ATM arrives. Determine the probability density function (PDF) of Y_4 and find its expected value E[Y_4].
