## Problem 3
Buses arrive at a stop according to a Poisson process with rate $\lambda = 4$ per hour. You are the 36th person in line, and each person boards independently (one bus per person).

**Questions:**

(a) Find $E[Y_{36}]$ and $\text{Var}(Y_{36})$, where $Y_{36}$ is the time until your bus arrives.

(b) Use the CLT to approximate $P(Y_{36} > 10)$.

(c) Use the CLT to approximate $P(8 \le Y_{36} \le 11)$.
