## Problem 1

A Poisson process has rate

$$
\lambda = 3 \text{ per hour}.
$$

Find:

1. The probability that there are exactly $0$ arrivals during $2$ hours.
2. The mean and variance of the number of arrivals during $2$ hours.

---

## Solution

(1) 

$$
P(N_2=0)=e^{-6}.
$$

(2)

$$
E[N_2]=6,
\qquad
\mathrm{Var}(N_2)=6.
$$
