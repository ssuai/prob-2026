# Hard Problem: Splitting a Poisson Process

Calls arrive at a service center according to a Poisson process with rate 12 calls per hour.

Each call is independently classified as a billing call with probability 0.25 and a technical support call with probability 0.75.

1. What is the rate of billing calls?
2. What is the probability that exactly 2 billing calls arrive in the next hour?
3. What is the probability that the first billing call arrives before the first technical support call?
4. What is the expected waiting time until the first call of any type arrives?
