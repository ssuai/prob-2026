# Problem 3: AI Cluster Race and Competing Exponentials

**Problem Statement:**
You are training a deep learning model on a shared university computing cluster. Your model successfully completes and saves "training checkpoints" according to a Poisson process with a rate of $\lambda_c = 3$ per hour. Simultaneously, the cluster experiences random "memory faults" (which interrupt jobs) according to an independent Poisson process with a rate of $\lambda_f = 2$ per hour.

You need to reach exactly $2$ checkpoints to finalize your progress. However, if the server experiences $3$ memory faults before you reach your 2nd checkpoint, your job is completely terminated by the system.

**Your Task:**
1. **Part A:** Find the probability that your model successfully finalizes its progress (reaches $2$ checkpoints before hitting $3$ faults).
2. **Part B:** Let $T$ be the total time (in hours) spent on the cluster until the job either successfully finishes or is terminated. Find the expected value of $T$, $\mathbb{E}[T]$.
