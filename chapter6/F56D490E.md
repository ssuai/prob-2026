### [Easy Level] Question 1: Network Packet Error Detection (15 Points)

A router in a data center transmits data in discrete packets. Due to physical noise in the transmission medium, each packet has an independent probability of $p = 0.1$ of being corrupted upon arrival. The sequence of packet transmissions is modeled as a Bernoulli process.

**(1-a)** Calculate the probability that exactly 2 out of the next 10 transmitted packets are corrupted. (Leave your answer in combination and power form; do not compute the final decimal value).

**(1-b)** Let $T$ be the total number of packets transmitted up to (and including) the very first corrupted packet. Calculate the exact expected value, $E[T]$, and the exact variance, $\text{Var}(T)$.
