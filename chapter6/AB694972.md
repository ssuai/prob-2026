# Problem 2 — Intermediate

**Topic:** Merging of Poisson Processes + Poisson PMF

---

## Problem

A coffee shop is located near two university buildings, A and B.

- Students from building A arrive according to a Poisson process with rate **λ_A = 4 customers/hour**.
- Students from building B arrive according to a Poisson process with rate **λ_B = 6 customers/hour**.
- The two arrival streams are **independent**.

The shop owner decides to model all arriving customers together.

**(a)** What is the distribution of the total number of customers arriving in any given hour? State the type of distribution and its parameter.

**(b)** What is the probability that **exactly 8 customers** arrive during a 30-minute window?

**(c)** Each arriving customer independently decides to order a hot drink with probability **p = 0.4**.  
What is the arrival rate of customers who order hot drinks?  
What type of process do they form, and why?
