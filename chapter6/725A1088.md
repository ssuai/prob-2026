# Medium Problem: Exponential Waiting Times

## Scenario
Customers arrive at a help desk according to a Poisson process with rate

\[
\lambda = 3 \text{ customers per hour}
\]

You begin observing the help desk at 12:00 PM.

## Questions

1. What is the probability that the first customer arrives after 12:40 PM?

2. If by 12:30 PM no customers have arrived, what is the probability that the first customer arrives before 12:50 PM?
