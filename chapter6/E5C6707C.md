1. easy

A smart vending machine checks every minute whether a customer buys a drink.
In each minute, the probability that at least one drink is bought is 0.08.
Assume that each minute is independent of the others.

Let Xi = 1 if a drink is bought during the i-th minute, and Xi = 0 otherwise.

1. What type of stochastic process is {Xi}?
2. Let S be the number of minutes with a purchase during the first 20 minutes. Find P(S = 3).
3. Let T be the number of minutes until the first purchase occurs. Find P(T = 5).
4. Find E[S] and E[T].
