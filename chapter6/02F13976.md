## Problem 1 (Easy)

A 24/7 technical support hotline receives customer inquiries according to a Poisson process with an average arrival rate of 4.5 calls per hour. The system administrator wants to evaluate the volume of traffic during the overnight shift to ensure that the team is adequately staffed. Assuming the arrival rate remains completely constant throughout the night, calculate the exact probability that the technical support hotline will receive exactly 10 calls during a continuous 2-hour period.
