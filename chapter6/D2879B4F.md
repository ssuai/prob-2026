# Problem 1 (Easy)

A help desk checks for new support tickets once every minute. In each minute, a new ticket arrives with probability $p=0.2$, independently of all other minutes. Let $N$ be the number of minutes with at least one new ticket during the next 5 minutes.

Task: Find $P(N=2)$.

---

# Solution

This is a Bernoulli process with 5 independent slots and success probability $0.2$.

Thus,

$$
N \sim \operatorname{Binomial}(5,0.2).
$$

Therefore,

$$
P(N=2)=\binom{5}{2}(0.2)^2(0.8)^3.
$$

So,

$$
P(N=2)=10(0.04)(0.512)=0.2048.
$$
