# Problem 3

In a Bernoulli process with success probability

$$
p=0.4,
$$

find:

1. The probability that the 5th success occurs on the 12th trial
2. The expected number of trials until the 5th success

It is okay to leave the answers in expression form.

---

# Solution

(1) 

$$
P(Y_5=12)
=
330(0.4)^5(0.6)^7
$$

(2)

$$
E[Y_5]
=
\frac{5}{p}
=
\frac{5}{0.4}
=
12.5
$$
