# Exercise 1: Poisson Process Basics (Easy)

**Topic:** The Poisson Process and PMF Calculations

**Problem:**
A local store experiences customer arrivals that can be modeled as a Poisson process with a rate of $\lambda = 10$ arrivals per minute.

1.  What is the expected number of customer arrivals in a 5-minute interval?
2.  Calculate the exact probability that there will be exactly 0 customer arrivals during a 1-minute interval.
