# Problem 3. Poisson Splitting and Erlang Waiting Time

A cloud monitoring system receives alerts according to a Poisson process with rate

$$
\lambda = 10
$$

alerts per hour.

Each alert is independently classified as critical with probability

$$
p = 0.2.
$$

Let $Y_4$ be the time until the 4th critical alert arrives.

Answer the following questions.

1. Find the rate of the critical-alert process.

2. Find the distribution of $Y_4$.

3. Find the expected value and variance of $Y_4$.

4. Find the probability that the 4th critical alert arrives within 3 hours.

$$
P(Y_4 \le 3)
$$

Show your work clearly.
