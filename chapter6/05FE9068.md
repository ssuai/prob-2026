# Problem 1: Shop Reroll and the Memoryless Property

**Problem Statement:**
You are playing an auto-battler game and desperately need one specific champion to complete your team composition. Every time you reroll the shop, there is an independent $10\%$ chance ($p = 0.10$) of that specific champion appearing. 

You have already rerolled the shop $5$ times in a row, and the champion hasn't appeared. 

**Your Task:**
Given this streak of bad luck, find the conditional probability that it will take more than $3$ additional rerolls to finally find your champion. Explain how the properties of the Bernoulli process apply to this scenario.
