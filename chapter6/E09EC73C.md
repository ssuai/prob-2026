## Problem 2 (Intermediate)
A campus parking gate records whether a VIP vehicle arrives during each 30-second slot. Each slot is independent of the others.

- Let $X_i=1$ if a VIP vehicle arrives during slot $i$, and $X_i=0$ otherwise.
- The sequence $X_1,X_2,\ldots$ is a **Bernoulli process** with parameter **$p=0.18$**.
- Let $Y_4$ be the slot number in which the **4th VIP vehicle** arrives.

Determine the **probability that the 4th VIP vehicle arrives in slot 12**, that is, $P(Y_4=12)$.
