# Problem 2 (Intermediate)

Customer orders arrive at an online shopping mall following a Poisson process at a rate of 4 orders per minute. Each order is independently classified as "express delivery" with probability 0.25, and "standard delivery" with probability 0.75.

(a) What processes do express and standard delivery orders each follow? Find each rate. Are the two processes independent of each other?

(b) Starting from an arbitrary observation point, find the probability of waiting more than 3 minutes for the next express delivery order.

(c) Find the expected value and variance of the time until the first 5 express delivery orders have all arrived.
