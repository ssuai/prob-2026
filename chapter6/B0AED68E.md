# Probability and Statistics Practice Problems

## Problem 1
A baseball player gets a hit with probability $p = 0.35$ each at-bat, independently. Let $T$ be the number of at-bats until the first hit (inclusive).

**Questions:**

(a) Find $P(T = 4)$, $E[T]$, and $\text{Var}(T)$.

(b) In a game with 5 at-bats, find the probability of getting at least 2 hits.

(c) Given that the first 2 at-bats were outs, find the probability that the first hit comes on the 5th at-bat.
