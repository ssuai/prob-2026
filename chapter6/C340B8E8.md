# Bernoulli Process — Intermediate Problem

## Problem 2

A computer system processes tasks in discrete time slots. In each slot, a priority task arrives with probability **p = 0.4**, independently of other slots. A non-priority task is executed only when no priority task is present.

Let **B** be the length (number of slots) of the first busy period, and **I** be the length of the first idle period.

**(a)** What is the PMF, mean, and variance of **B**?

**(b)** What is the PMF, mean, and variance of **I**?

**(c)** Given that the first busy period has already lasted 3 slots with no idle slot yet, what is the probability that it lasts **at least 2 more slots**?

**(d)** Two independent computer systems A and B operate as described above, with parameters **p_A = 0.3** and **p_B = 0.5** respectively. Their task streams are merged into a single system. What is the probability of a busy slot in the merged system?
