## Problem 2

A Poisson process has rate

$$
\lambda = 5 \text{ per hour}.
$$

Each arrival is independently kept with probability

$$
p=0.4,
$$

and discarded otherwise.

During one hour, find:

1. The rate of the kept process
2. The rate of the discarded process
3. The probability that exactly $1$ arrival is kept and exactly $2$ arrivals are discarded

It is okay to leave the answers in expression form.

---

## Solution

(1), (2)

$$
\lambda_{\text{kept}} = 5(0.4)=2,
\qquad
\lambda_{\text{discard}} = 5(0.6)=3.
$$

(3)

$$
P(\text{kept}=1,\ \text{discard}=2)
=
\left(2e^{-2}\right)
\left(\frac92 e^{-3}\right)
=
9e^{-5}
$$
