# Problem 2 — Intermediate

## Topic: Poisson Process and Exponential Waiting Time

Calls arrive at a customer service center according to a Poisson process with rate

\[
\lambda=4 \text{ calls per hour}.
\]

Let \(N_t\) be the number of calls during an interval of length \(t\) hours, and let \(T\) be the time until the first call arrives.

Answer the following questions.

1. Find the probability that exactly 6 calls arrive in 2 hours.
2. Find the probability that no call arrives in the first 30 minutes.
3. Find the probability that the first call arrives after 15 minutes.
4. Find \(E[T]\) and \(Var(T)\).
