# Exercise 3: Erlang Distribution and CLT (Hard)

**Topic:** Erlang PDF and Central Limit Theorem Approximation

**Problem:**
You are 56th in a queue for a hotline. Departures follow a Poisson process with rate $\lambda = 2$ per minute. Let $Y$ be your total waiting time.

1.  Identify the exact probability distribution of $Y$.
2.  Set up the exact integral to calculate the probability that you wait for at least 30 minutes, $P(Y \ge 30)$.
3.  Use the Central Limit Theorem (CLT) to provide a numerical approximation for $P(Y \ge 30)$.
