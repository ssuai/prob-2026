# [Easy] Problem 1: KakaoTalk Group Chat Vote & Memorylessness

## 1. Problem Scenario
Jimin and her 4 college friends (5 people in total) are trying to decide on a meetup time for this weekend using a vote in their KakaoTalk group chat. Because everyone has a busy schedule, they check the chat and decide whether to complete their vote exactly on the hour (1:00 PM, 2:00 PM, 3:00 PM, etc.).

Based on past history, the probability that all 5 members successfully complete their votes and the meetup time becomes finalized at any given hour is p = 0.2, independently of all other hours. If the vote is incomplete, they wait until the next hour to try again.

## 2. Questions

### (a)
Let T be the number of hourly trials up to and including the hour when the meetup time is finally fixed (T ~ Geometric(p=0.2)).

Find the probability that Jimin has to wait for at least 4 hours, which is:

P(T > 3)

using the properties of the Geometric PMF.

### (b)
Suppose that 1:00 PM, 2:00 PM, and 3:00 PM have already passed, and the vote is still not finalized.

Find the conditional probability that they will wait for 2 more hours, meaning the success occurs exactly at the 5th hour (5:00 PM), given no success in the first 3 hours.

Explain how this result connects to the Memorylessness Property using mathematical expressions.
