# Problem 1 — Easy

**Topic:** Poisson Process — Number of Arrivals (Poisson PMF) and First Arrival Time (Exponential)

---

## Problem

A university help desk receives student questions according to a Poisson process with an average rate of **λ = 3 questions per hour**.

**(a)** What is the probability that **exactly 2 questions** arrive during a 1-hour period?

**(b)** What is the probability that **no questions** arrive during a 30-minute period?

**(c)** What is the **expected time** (in minutes) until the first question arrives after the help desk opens?
