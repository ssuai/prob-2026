# Exercise 2: Competing Exponentials (Intermediate)

**Topic:** Memorylessness and Sum of Exponentials

**Problem:**
A light fixture has three bulbs. All three bulbs have independent and identically distributed (i.i.d.) exponential lifetimes, each with parameter $\lambda$.

1.  What is the distribution and the rate of the time until the *first* bulb burns out?
2.  Let $T_1$ be the time until the first burnout, $T_2$ be the time between the first and second burnout, and $T_3$ be the time between the second and third burnout. What is the total expected time until all three bulbs have burned out, $\mathbb{E}[T_1 + T_2 + T_3]$?
