# Easy Problem: Merged Bernoulli Trials

## Scenario
Two sensors, A and B, detect motion in a hallway independently during discrete one-second intervals.

- Sensor A detects motion with probability \( p_A = 0.3 \)
- Sensor B detects motion with probability \( p_B = 0.2 \)

An alarm is triggered if at least one sensor detects motion during a given second.

## Question
What is the probability that exactly 2 alarms are triggered during a 5-second period?
