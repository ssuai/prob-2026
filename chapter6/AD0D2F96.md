### [Medium Level] Question 2: AI Server Crash Recovery (20 Points)

An AI training server cluster experiences critical crashes according to a Poisson process with a rate of $\lambda = 0.2$ crashes per hour. Let $T$ denote the time until the next crash.

**(2-a)** Calculate the conditional probability that the server runs without a crash for a total of 8 hours, given that it has already run perfectly for the first 5 hours (i.e., calculate $P(T > 8 \mid T > 5)$). Show your explicit mathematical steps and leave your answer in exponential form.

**(2-b)** The data center's maintenance policy dictates that the server hardware will be entirely replaced immediately after the 3rd crash occurs. Calculate the exact expected total time (in hours) from a fresh start until the server hardware is replaced.
