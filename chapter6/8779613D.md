3. Hard
An automated manufacturing system relies on three parallel, critical machine components running simultaneously. The lifetimes of these components, denoted as T_1, T_2, and T_3, are independent and identically distributed (i.i.d.) exponential random variables, each with a failure rate parameter $lambda. The system operates smoothly as long as all three components are functioning. However, the system experiences its first minor fault when any one of the components fails, a second fault when the next component fails, and a total system shutdown when the final remaining component fails. Derive the total expected duration E[T_{total}] from the initial startup until the absolute total shutdown of the system, expressing your answer in terms of $lambda.
