**# Problem 3 (Hard Level)**

**## Topic: Bernoulli Splitting + Pascal PMF**



**### Problem Statement**

**Data packets arrive at a network router according to a Bernoulli process with a success probability of $p = 0.6$ per time slot.** 



**Each arriving packet is independently inspected and classified:**

**- With probability $q = 0.5$, it is labeled as "High Priority".**

**- With probability $1-q = 0.5$, it is labeled as "Low Priority".**



**### Question**

**Find the probability that the 3rd "High Priority" packet arrives exactly at the 5th time slot.**
