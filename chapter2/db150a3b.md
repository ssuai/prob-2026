## 🔴 3️⃣ Hard Problem 

Two random variables ( X, Y ) have the following joint PMF:

$$
p_{X,Y}(x, y) =
\begin{cases}
c(x + y), & x = 0,1,2,; y = 0,1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

(1)

Find the value of the constant $c$.

(2)

Find the marginal PMF of $X$, $p_X(x)$.

(3)

Find the marginal PMF of $Y$, $p_Y(y)$.

(4)

Find

$$
P(X = 1 \mid Y = 1)
$$

(5) 

Are $X$ and $Y$ independent?
(Determine using the definition and explain your reasoning.)

(6) 

Find

$$
E[X], \quad E[Y]
$$

(7)  

Find

$$
\mathrm{Var}(X)
$$

---

Answer: 

(1) $$c : 1/9$$

(2) 
$$
p_X(x) =
\begin{cases}
1/9 & \text{if } x=0 \\
1/3 & \text{if } x=1 \\
5/9 & \text{if } x=2 \\
0 & otherwise
\end{cases}
$$

(3)
$$
p_Y(y) =
\begin{cases}
1/3 & \text{if } y=0 \\
2/3 & \text{if } x=1 \\
0 & otherwise
\end{cases}
$$

(4)

$$2/9$$

(5)
$$\text{not independence}$$
(6)
$$13/9$$
(7)
$$5/3$$
(8)
$$38/81$$
