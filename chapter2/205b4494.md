Problem:
Consider two discrete random variables, $X$ and $Y$, representing the performance of two different modules in a system architecture. Their joint PMF $p_{X,Y}(x,y)$ is given by the following table:
y|x    1        2         3
2      0.1      0.2       0.1
1      0.2      0.3       0.1 
1.Determine the marginal probability mass functions $p_X(x)$ and $p_Y(y)$.
2.Calculate the expected value of the sum of the variables, $E[X + Y]$
3.Calculate the variance of $Y$, denoted as $var(Y)$.
