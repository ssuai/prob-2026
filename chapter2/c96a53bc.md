The joint PMF of two random variables X and Y is given by the following table:

| Y / X | 1 | 2 | 3 |
| **1** | 0.1 | 0.2 | 0.1 |
| **2** | 0.2 | 0.1 | 0.3 |

1. Find the marginal PMF of X, p_X(x), through marginalization.
2. Let Z = X + Y. Calculate the expectation E[Z] using the linearity of expectation (E[Z] = E[X] + E[Y]).
