### Easy :   Coin Toss and Bernoulli Random Variable

Consider a biased coin where the probability of landing on heads (H) is $0.6$ and tails (T) is $0.4$. Define a random variable $X$ such that $X=1$ if the result is heads and $X=0$ if it is tails.

1. Write the Probability Mass Function (PMF) $p_X(x)$ of $X$.
2. Calculate the expectation $E[X]$.
3. Calculate the variance $var(X)$.
