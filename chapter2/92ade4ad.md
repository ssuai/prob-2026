# Problem 1 — Discrete Random Variable & Expectation

## Problem Statement

A biased coin is flipped repeatedly until the first time two consecutive heads (HH) appear.  
Let \( X \) be the number of flips required.

- Describe the possible values of \( X \).
- Compute \( E[X] \).

---

## Assumptions

- Each flip is independent.
- \( P(\text{Head}) = p \), \( P(\text{Tail}) = 1 - p \), where \( 0 < p < 1 \).
