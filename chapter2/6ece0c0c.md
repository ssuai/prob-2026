**Q8.** A transmitter sends a message over a network. The message length Y (in bytes) has PMF:
 
$$p_Y(y) = \begin{cases} 3/4 & \text{if } y = 10^2 \\ 1/4 & \text{if } y = 10^4 \end{cases}$$
 
The travel time X given message length satisfies:
 
$$p_{X|Y}(x \mid 10^2) = \begin{cases} 1/2 & \text{if } x = 1 \\ 1/2 & \text{if } x = 2 \end{cases}, \qquad p_{X|Y}(x \mid 10^4) = \begin{cases} 1/3 & \text{if } x = 2 \\ 2/3 & \text{if } x = 5 \end{cases}$$
 
(a) Compute the joint PMF $p_{X,Y}(x,y)$ for all $(x, y)$.
 
(b) Find the marginal PMF $p_X(x)$.
 
(c) Compute $E[X]$.
