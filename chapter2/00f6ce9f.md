### Problem 1 - Easy
Let $X$ be a discrete random variable representing the processing time of a computational task. 
Suppose the expected value is $E[X] = 5$ ms and the variance is $var(X) = 2$ ms².
A new random variable $Y$ is defined as $Y = 3X + 10$. Calculate the mean $E[Y]$ and the variance $var(Y)$.
