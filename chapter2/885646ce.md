# Probability Mass Function (PMF) Practice Problem: Intermediate

## Problem: Dice Differences
You roll two fair 6-sided dice simultaneously. Let $X$ be the **absolute difference** between the two numbers rolled (e.g., if you roll a 2 and a 5, $X = |2 - 5| = 3$).

* **Task:** Determine the PMF of $X$ for all possible values of $x$.
