# Damaged Items in a Shipment

**Difficulty:** Intermediate  
**Main Topic:** Joint PMF, marginal PMFs, conditional PMF, and expectation of a function of two random variables

A company ships small boxes containing electronic accessories. Let \(X\) be the number of wireless earbuds in a box and let \(Y\) be the number of damaged earbuds in that same box. The joint probability mass function of \((X,Y)\) is given by the table below.

| \(y \\ x\) | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| 0 | 0.18 | 0.24 | 0.12 |
| 1 | 0 | 0.16 | 0.20 |
| 2 | 0 | 0 | 0.10 |

Any probability not shown in the table is 0.

1. Find the marginal PMF \(p_X(x)\).
2. Find the marginal PMF \(p_Y(y)\).
3. Find the conditional PMF \(p_{Y|X}(y\mid 2)\).
4. Let \(Z=X+Y\). Compute \(E[Z]\).
