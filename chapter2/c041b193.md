## Problem

### Korean

공정한 동전을 세 번 독립적으로 던지는 실험을 고려하자. 표본 공간은

$$\Omega = \{HHH,\; HHT,\; HTH,\; HTT,\; THH,\; THT,\; TTH,\; TTT\}$$

이며, 각 결과의 확률은 $\frac{1}{8}$로 동일하다. 두 확률변수를 다음과 같이 정의한다.

$$X = \text{앞면(H)이 나온 총 횟수}, \qquad Y = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{첫 번째 던지기가 앞면인 경우,} \\ 0 & \text{그 외.} \end{cases}$$

**(1)** 결합 확률질량함수(joint PMF) $p_{X,Y}(x, y)$를 표(table) 형태로 구하여라.

**(2)** $X$와 $Y$ 각각의 주변 PMF(marginal PMF) $p_X(x)$와 $p_Y(y)$를 구하여라.

**(3)** $X$와 $Y$가 독립인지 판단하여라. 독립의 정의

$$p_{X,Y}(x, y) = p_X(x)\,p_Y(y) \quad \text{(모든 } x, y \text{에 대해)}$$

를 이용하고, 구체적인 반례(counter-example) 또는 완전한 검증을 제시하여라.

**(4)** 전체 기댓값 정리(total expectation theorem)를 이용하여 $E[X]$를 계산하여라. 즉,

$$E[X] = E[X \mid Y=0]\,P(Y=0) + E[X \mid Y=1]\,P(Y=1)$$

의 형태로 전개하여라.

---

### English

Consider the experiment of tossing a fair coin three times independently. The sample space is

$$\Omega = \{HHH,\; HHT,\; HTH,\; HTT,\; THH,\; THT,\; TTH,\; TTT\},$$

with each outcome having probability $\frac{1}{8}$. Define two random variables:

$$X = \text{total number of heads}, \qquad Y = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{if the first toss is heads,} \\ 0 & \text{otherwise.} \end{cases}$$

**(1)** Find the joint PMF $p_{X,Y}(x, y)$ and display it as a table.

**(2)** Find the marginal PMFs $p_X(x)$ and $p_Y(y)$.

**(3)** Determine whether $X$ and $Y$ are independent. Use the definition

$$p_{X,Y}(x, y) = p_X(x)\,p_Y(y) \quad \text{for all } x, y,$$

and provide either a specific counterexample or a complete verification.

**(4)** Use the total expectation theorem to compute $E[X]$ by writing

$$E[X] = E[X \mid Y=0]\,P(Y=0) + E[X \mid Y=1]\,P(Y=1).$$

---

## Solution

> **[Step 1] Model Setup**
>
> The sample space has $|\Omega| = 8$ equally likely outcomes. For each outcome $\omega \in \Omega$:
> - $X(\omega)$ counts the number of H's.
> - $Y(\omega) = 1$ if the first character of $\omega$ is H, else $Y(\omega) = 0$.
>
> The possible values are $X \in \{0,1,2,3\}$ and $Y \in \{0,1\}$.

---

> **[Step 2] Key Observation / Diagram**
>
> Partition $\Omega$ by the value of $Y$:
>
> | $Y = 1$ (first toss H) | $Y = 0$ (first toss T) |
> |---|---|
> | HHH $(X=3)$ | THH $(X=2)$ |
> | HHT $(X=2)$ | THT $(X=1)$ |
> | HTH $(X=2)$ | TTH $(X=1)$ |
> | HTT $(X=1)$ | TTT $(X=0)$ |
>
> Each sub-group contains exactly 4 outcomes, confirming $P(Y=1) = P(Y=0) = \frac{1}{2}$.

---

> **[Step 3] Derivation**
>
> **Part (1) — Joint PMF.**
>
> Count outcomes for each $(x, y)$ pair:
>
> | | $y = 0$ | $y = 1$ | Row sum |
> |---|---|---|---|
> | $x = 0$ | $\frac{1}{8}$ (TTT) | $0$ | $\frac{1}{8}$ |
> | $x = 1$ | $\frac{2}{8}$ (THT, TTH) | $\frac{1}{8}$ (HTT) | $\frac{3}{8}$ |
> | $x = 2$ | $\frac{1}{8}$ (THH) | $\frac{2}{8}$ (HHT, HTH) | $\frac{3}{8}$ |
> | $x = 3$ | $0$ | $\frac{1}{8}$ (HHH) | $\frac{1}{8}$ |
> | **Col sum** | $\frac{4}{8} = \frac{1}{2}$ | $\frac{4}{8} = \frac{1}{2}$ | $1$ |
>
> So the joint PMF is:
>
> $$p_{X,Y}(x,y) = \begin{cases} \frac{1}{8} & (x,y) \in \{(0,0),(1,1),(2,0),(3,1)\}, \\ \frac{2}{8} & (x,y) \in \{(1,0),(2,1)\}, \\ 0 & \text{otherwise.} \end{cases}$$
>
> ---
>
> **Part (2) — Marginal PMFs.**
>
> Sum the joint PMF along each axis:
>
> $$p_X(0) = \frac{1}{8}, \quad p_X(1) = \frac{3}{8}, \quad p_X(2) = \frac{3}{8}, \quad p_X(3) = \frac{1}{8}.$$
>
> This is the **Binomial$(3, \frac{1}{2})$** PMF, consistent with three fair coin tosses.
>
> $$p_Y(0) = \frac{1}{2}, \qquad p_Y(1) = \frac{1}{2}.$$
>
> ---
>
> **Part (3) — Independence Check.**
>
> Test the factorization condition at $(x, y) = (0, 1)$:
>
> $$p_{X,Y}(0,1) = 0,$$
> $$p_X(0)\,p_Y(1) = \frac{1}{8} \cdot \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{16}.$$
>
> Since $0 \neq \frac{1}{16}$, the factorization fails. **$X$ and $Y$ are not independent.**
>
> Intuitively, $Y = 1$ guarantees at least one head, so $X \geq 1$ whenever $Y = 1$, meaning $Y$ provides information about $X$.

---

> **[Step 4] Computation**
>
> **Part (4) — Total Expectation.**
>
> First, compute $E[X \mid Y = y]$ using the conditional PMF
> $p_{X \mid Y}(x \mid y) = p_{X,Y}(x,y) / p_Y(y)$:
>
> **Conditioned on $Y = 0$** (first toss is T; the remaining two tosses are i.i.d. fair):
>
> $$p_{X \mid Y}(0 \mid 0) = \frac{1/8}{1/2} = \frac{1}{4}, \quad p_{X \mid Y}(1 \mid 0) = \frac{2/8}{1/2} = \frac{2}{4}, \quad p_{X \mid Y}(2 \mid 0) = \frac{1/8}{1/2} = \frac{1}{4}.$$
>
> $$E[X \mid Y=0] = 0 \cdot \frac{1}{4} + 1 \cdot \frac{2}{4} + 2 \cdot \frac{1}{4} = \frac{0+2+2}{4} = 1.$$
>
> **Conditioned on $Y = 1$** (first toss is H; that contributes 1 head, plus Binomial$(2,\frac{1}{2})$ from the remaining two):
>
> $$p_{X \mid Y}(1 \mid 1) = \frac{1}{4}, \quad p_{X \mid Y}(2 \mid 1) = \frac{2}{4}, \quad p_{X \mid Y}(3 \mid 1) = \frac{1}{4}.$$
>
> $$E[X \mid Y=1] = 1 \cdot \frac{1}{4} + 2 \cdot \frac{2}{4} + 3 \cdot \frac{1}{4} = \frac{1+4+3}{4} = 2.$$
>
> **Apply the total expectation theorem:**
>
> $$E[X] = E[X \mid Y=0]\,P(Y=0) + E[X \mid Y=1]\,P(Y=1) = 1 \cdot \frac{1}{2} + 2 \cdot \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{2}.$$

---

> **[Step 5] Verification**
>
> - All joint PMF entries are non-negative and sum to 1. ✓
> - $E[X] = \frac{3}{2}$, consistent with the Binomial$(3, \frac{1}{2})$ mean $np = 3 \cdot \frac{1}{2} = \frac{3}{2}$. ✓
> - $p_Y(0) + p_Y(1) = 1$. ✓

---

**Answers:**

$$\boxed{E[X] = \frac{3}{2}}.$$

$X$ and $Y$ are **not independent**, as demonstrated by the counterexample $p_{X,Y}(0,1) = 0 \neq \frac{1}{16} = p_X(0)\,p_Y(1)$.
