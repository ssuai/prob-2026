## Problem 2 (Intermediate)
A cloud computing provider monitors the number of active virtual machines ($X$) on a specific server node. The operational cost ($Y$) is a non-linear function of the number of active machines due to cooling and power overheads.
- The Probability Mass Function (PMF) of $X$ is given by:$p_X(x) = \begin{cases} 0.2 & x = 1 \\ 0.5 & x = 2 \\ 0.3 & x = 3 \end{cases}$
- The cost function is defined as: $g(X) = 5X^2 + 10$
- $Y = g(X)$ is the random variable representing the total cost in dollars.

Determine the expected value of the operational cost, **$E[Y]$**.
