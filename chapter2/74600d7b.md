# Intermediate Problem — Joint Distribution and Conditional Probability


## Scenario
A factory produces items from two machines, A and B.


Machine A produces 60% of the items, and Machine B produces 40%.

Each item can be defective (D) or non-defective (N).

- P(D | A) = 0.1
- P(D | B) = 0.3

Let:
X = machine used (A or B)  
Y = product quality (D or N)

---

## Problem

1. Find the joint PMF P(X, Y).  
2. Compute P(A | D).
