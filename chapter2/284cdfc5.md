The energy management system of a smart building records the number of times a heavy-duty air conditioner is activated per hour as a discrete random variable X. The Probability Mass Function (PMF) p_X(x) of X is given below:
p_X(0) = 0.2
p_X(1) = 0.4
p_X(2) = 0.3
p_X(3) = 0.1
Let the hourly power consumption (in kWh) of this air conditioner be a random variable Y, which is related to X by the linear equation Y = 5X + 2. Calculate the following:
(a) The expected value E[X] and the variance $\text{var}(X)$ of the air conditioner activations.
(b) The expected value E[Y] and the variance $\text{var}(Y)$ of the hourly power consumption.
