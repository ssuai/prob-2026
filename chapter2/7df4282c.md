## Problem
An e-commerce company sells two categories of smart home devices: Category A and Category B. The daily number of units sold for Category A is modeled as a binomial random variable $X$ with $n = 100$ and $p = 0.1$. The daily number of units sold for Category B is modeled as a Poisson random variable $Y$ with parameter $\lambda = 5$. The sales of $X$ and $Y$ are independent of each other. 
The company makes a net profit of \$50 for each Category A device and \$80 for each Category B device. The daily fixed server operation cost is \$100. Let $Z$ be the total daily profit in dollars, defined as $Z = 50X + 80Y - 100$.
Calculate the expected daily profit $E[Z]$ and the variance of the daily profit $Var[Z]$.
