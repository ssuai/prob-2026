(Hard) Sample Mean, Unbiasedness, Variance Behavior
Problem
X₁,…,Xₙ are i.i.d. Bernoulli(p), and Sₙ = (1/n)ΣXᵢ.

(a) Compute E[Sₙ] and show that it is an unbiased estimator of p.
(b) Compute var(Sₙ) and describe how it behaves as n increases.
(c) What happens to the variance if the sample size is doubled?
