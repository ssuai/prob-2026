An IT customer support center analyzes technical support tickets. Let random variable $X$ represent the severity of a ticket (1: Normal, 2: Urgent), and random variable $Y$ represent the resolution time (1: Same-day, 2: Delayed). The joint PMF $p_{X,Y}(x,y)$ is given as follows:
$p_{X,Y}(1,1) = 0.4$
$p_{X,Y}(1,2) = 0.1$
$p_{X,Y}(2,1) = 0.2$
$p_{X,Y}(2,2) = 0.3$

(a) Find the marginal PMFs $p_X(x)$ and $p_Y(y)$.
(b) Are the random variables $X$ and $Y$ independent? Prove your answer logically using mathematical formulas.
(c) Calculate the expected value $E[XY]$.
