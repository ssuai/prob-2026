# Hard Problem (Highest Difficulty)

Two random variables X and Y are defined as follows.

PMF of Y:
- p_Y(0) = 1/2
- p_Y(1) = 1/2

Conditional PMF:
- p_{X|Y}(x|0) = 1/3 for x ∈ {-1, 0, 1}
- p_{X|Y}(x|1) = 1/6 for x ∈ {-2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3}

Define a new random variable Z = X + Y.

Derive the PMF of Z, compute E[Z] and var(Z), and determine whether X and Y are independent. Justify your answer.
