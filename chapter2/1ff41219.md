**Problem:**
 
A student is practicing for the TOPIK II exam and focuses on learning new vocabulary. Suppose the student attempts to memorize $n=6$ difficult words. For each word, the probability of successfully remembering it is $p=0.75$, independent of other words. Let the random variable $X$ denote the total number of words the student remembers correctly.
 

1.  Identify the distribution of the random variable $X$ and state its Probability Mass Function (PMF).
     
2.  Calculate the expected number of words the student will remember ($E\[X\]$).
     
3.  Find the probability that the student remembers all 6 words.
