## 🟡 2️⃣ Medium Problem

A coin is tossed 3 times. The probability of heads on each to toss is $p$.

- $X$ : total number of heads
- $Y$ : number of heads in the first two tosses

(1) Find the PMF of $X$.

---

(2) Find the conditional probability: $$P(X = 2∣Y = 1)$$

---

Answer:

(1)
$$
P(X = x) =
\begin{cases}
(1-p)^3 & \text{if } x=0 \\
(1-p)^2 p & \text{if } x=1 \\
(1-p) p^2 & \text{if } x=2 \\
p^3 & \text{if } x=3
\end{cases}
$$
