# Digital Coupon Rewards

**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Main Topic:** PMF, expectation, variance, and a function of a random variable

A convenience store app gives a customer a random number of digital coupons after a purchase. Let \(X\) be the number of coupons received, with probability mass function

\[
p_X(x)=
\begin{cases}
0.10, & x=0,\\
0.25, & x=1,\\
0.40, & x=2,\\
0.25, & x=3,\\
0, & \text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}
\]

The app also defines a bonus score by \(Y=X+2\).

1. Compute \(E[X]\).
2. Compute \(\mathrm{var}(X)\).
3. Find the probability mass function of \(Y\).
