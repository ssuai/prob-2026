# Two Inspection Scores

**Difficulty:** Hard  
**Main Topic:** Joint PMF, conditional PMF, independence, and expectation involving two random variables

Two inspection scores are recorded for a product. Let \(X\) and \(Y\) be discrete random variables with joint probability mass function

\[
p_{X,Y}(x,y)=
\begin{cases}
c(x+y+1), & x \in \{0,1,2\},\ y \in \{0,1\},\\
0, & \text{otherwise.}
\end{cases}
\]

1. Find the value of the constant \(c\).
2. Find the conditional PMF \(p_{X|Y}(x\mid 1)\).
3. Determine whether \(X\) and \(Y\) are independent. 
4. Compute \(E[XY]\).
