### Intermediate : Analyzing Joint Probability Distribution

The joint PMF $p_{X,Y}(x, y)$ of two random variables $X$ and $Y$ is given by the following table:

| X \ Y | Y=1 | Y=2 |
| :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **X=0** | 0.2 | 0.1 |
| **X=1** | 0.4 | 0.3 |

1. Find the marginal PMFs $p_X(x)$ and $p_Y(y)$ for $X$ and $Y$.
2. Find $E[X+Y]$ using the linearity of expectation.
3. Determine whether $X$ and $Y$ are independent and explain your reasoning.
