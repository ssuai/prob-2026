## Problem 1 (Easy)
In a satellite communication system, a digital transmitter sends a burst of data packets to a ground station. 
Each packet is subject to atmospheric interference during transmission.
- Total packets sent in one burst: $n = 30$
- Probability of a single packet being successfully received: $p = 0.92$
- Each packet transmission is an independent Bernoulli trial.
- The random variable $X$ represents the total number of successfully received packets. 
Calculate the **variance of the total number of successfully received packets in a single burst**.
