## Problem 3 (Hard)
A security system monitors two different entry points, Gate A ($X$) and Gate B ($Y$), in a high-security facility. The number of unauthorized access attempts at each gate is recorded hourly and described by a Joint PMF.
- The Joint PMF $p_{X,Y}(x,y)$ is defined as:$p_{X,Y}(x,y) = \frac{x + y}{18}$ for $x \in \{1, 2\}$ and $y \in \{1, 2\}$.
- The random variables $X$ and $Y$ represent attempts at Gate A and Gate B respectively.

Calculate the conditional PMF of the number of attempts at Gate A given that exactly 2 attempts were detected at Gate B, denoted as $p_{X|Y}(x|2)$.
