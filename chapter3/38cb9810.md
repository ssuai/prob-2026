# Problem 3 (Hard)

Two continuous random variables $X$ and $Y$ have the joint PDF

$$
f_{X,Y}(x,y)=
\begin{cases}
2, & 0 \le x \le y \le 1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

Task: Find the conditional PDF $f_{X|Y}(x|y)$.

---

# Solution

We use

$$
f_{X|Y}(x|y)=\frac{f_{X,Y}(x,y)}{f_Y(y)}.
$$

So we first find the marginal PDF of $Y$.

For $0 \le y \le 1$,

$$
f_Y(y)=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_{X,Y}(x,y)\,dx
=\int_0^y 2\,dx
=2y.
$$

Thus,

$$
f_Y(y)=
\begin{cases}
2y, & 0 \le y \le 1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

Now for $0 \le x \le y \le 1$,

$$
f_{X|Y}(x|y)=\frac{f_{X,Y}(x,y)}{f_Y(y)}
=\frac{2}{2y}
=\frac{1}{y}.
$$

Therefore,

$$
f_{X|Y}(x|y)=
\begin{cases}
\frac{1}{y}, & 0 \le x \le y \le 1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$
