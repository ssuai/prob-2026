# Problem 1: Model Training Time

**Problem Statement:**
The time it takes for a basic machine learning model to complete one training epoch on a given dataset is uniformly distributed between 12 seconds and 20 seconds. Let continuous random variable $X$ represent the training time for a single epoch.

**Your Task:**
1. **Part A:** What is the probability that a randomly selected epoch takes less than 15 seconds to train? (Calculate using the Probability Density Function).
2. **Part B:** What is the expected (average) training time, $E[X]$, for one epoch?
