## 🟡 2️⃣ Medium Problem

Two random variables ( X, Y ) have the following joint PDF:

$$
f_{X,Y}(x, y) =
\begin{cases}
2, & 0 < x < y < 1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

(1)

Find $f_Y(y)$.

(2)

Find the conditional PDF $f_{X|Y}(x \mid y)$.

(3)

Find

$$
P\left(X < \tfrac{1}{2} \mid Y = \tfrac{3}{4}\right)
$$

(4) 

Are $X$ and $Y$ independent?

---

Answer:

(1) $2y$


(2) 
$\tfrac{1}{y}$

(3)
$\tfrac{2}{3}$

(4) **not independence**
