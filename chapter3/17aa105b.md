# Problem 3

**Difficulty:** Hard

Let \(T\) be an exponential random variable with mean 5. Also suppose that, given \(T=t\), the random variable \(Y\) is normally distributed with mean \(10+t\) and variance 1. In notation,

\[
Y\mid T=t \sim N(10+t,1).
\]

1. Find the pdf of \(T\).
2. Find the joint density \(f_{T,Y}(t,y)\).

**Main Topic:** Exponential distribution, normal conditional density, joint density construction
