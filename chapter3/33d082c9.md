\## 2. Intermediate Level

\*\*Topic: Joint PDF and Marginal PDF\*\* 



\*\*\[Problem]\*\*

Two continuous random variables $X$ and $Y$ have a joint PDF $f\_{X,Y}(x, y)$ defined as:

\*$f\_{X,Y}(x, y) = x + y$ for $0 \\le x \\le 1$ and $0 \\le y \\le 1$\*$f\_{X,Y}(x, y) = 0$ otherwise

1.Find the marginal PDF $f\_X(x)$ for the variable $X$. 

2\. Determine if $X$ and $Y$ are independent. \[cite\_start]Justify your answer using the relationship between $f\_{X,Y}(x, y)$ and $f\_X(x) \\cdot f\_Y(y)$.
