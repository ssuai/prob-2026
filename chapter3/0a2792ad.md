## Problem 1: AI Audio Quantization Error Analysis

**[Core Concept: Continuous Uniform Distribution]**

**[Problem Scenario]**
In an AI-based audio processing system, the quantization error $X$ of an analog-to-digital converter is modeled as a continuous random variable that is **uniformly distributed** over the interval $[0, 2]$. Determine the probability $P(X \ge 1.5)$ that the quantization noise level exceeds 1.5.
