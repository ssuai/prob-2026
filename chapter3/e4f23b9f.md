### [Intermediate] Standardization and Probability of Normal Distribution

**Problem:**
An employee's commute time $X$ (in minutes) follows a normal distribution with a mean $\mu = 30$ minutes and a standard deviation $\sigma = 5$ minutes. What is the probability that it takes 35 minutes or less for this employee to commute from home to work, $P(X \le 35)$? (Round to four decimal places using the standard normal distribution table value $\Phi(z)$.)
