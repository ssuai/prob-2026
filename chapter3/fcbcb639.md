Suppose that the true value X of a measurement follows a normal distribution with mean 5 and variance 4.

X ~ N(5,4)

The measurement error Z follows a normal distribution with mean 0 and variance 1, and X and Z are independent.

Z ~ N(0,1)

The observed value is defined by

Y = X + Z

Find the mean and variance of Y.
Identify the distribution of Y.
Express P(Y <= 6) using the standard normal distribution.
Standardize and express P(4 <= Y <= 7).
