# Problem 2: Shifted Triangular Support and CDF (Intermediate)

## 1. Problem Description
Two continuous random variables $X$ and $Y$ have a joint PDF defined over a triangular region starting at (1, 1) and bounded by the line $y=x$. The joint PDF is:

$$
f_{X,Y}(x,y) = 
\begin{cases} 
c, & 1 \le x \le 3, \ 1 \le y \le x \\ 
0, & \text{otherwise} 
\end{cases}
$$

## 2. Questions
1. Determine the constant $c$ using integration to ensure the total volume (probability) equals 1.
2. Find the marginal probability density function of $X$, $f_X(x)$.
3. Derive the joint cumulative distribution function (CDF), $F_{X,Y}(x,y)$, and verify whether $F_{X,Y}(3,3) = 1$ holds true.
