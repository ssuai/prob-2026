# intermediate Probability Problem (Continuous RV, Joint PDF, Normal & Exponential)
A smart traffic monitoring system measures vehicle speeds using a noisy
radar.

-   The true speed of a vehicle $X$ (km/h) follows an exponential
    distribution with mean 60:

    $$
    f_X(x) = \frac{1}{60} e^{-x/60}, \quad x \ge 0
    $$

-   The radar measurement is:

    $$
    Y = X + Z
    $$

    where noise $Z \sim \mathcal{N}(0, X^2/100)$.

Find the joint PDF $f_{X,Y}(x,y)$.
