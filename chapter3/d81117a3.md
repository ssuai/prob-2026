# Problem 1: Shifted Rectangular Support (Easy)

## 1. Problem Description
Consider two continuous random variables $X$ and $Y$ with a joint PDF defined over a rectangular region that does not start at the origin, beginning at the point (1, 2). The joint PDF is given by:

$$
f_{X,Y}(x,y) = 
\begin{cases} 
c, & 1 \le x \le 3, \ 2 \le y \le 5 \\ 
0, & \text{otherwise} 
\end{cases}
$$

## 2. Questions
1. Find the value of the constant $c$ such that the total probability over the support equals 1.
2. Derive the joint cumulative distribution function (CDF), $F_{X,Y}(x,y)$, for an arbitrary point $(x, y)$ within the support.
3. Mathematically prove whether the two random variables $X$ and $Y$ are independent.
