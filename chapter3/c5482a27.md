# Easy Problem — 2D Uniform Joint PDF

## Problem

Alice and Bob agree to meet at a coffee shop. Each arrives at a uniformly random time between 0 and 1 hour. Let $X$ and $Y$ denote their arrival times respectively, with joint PDF:

$$f_{X,Y}(x,y) = 1, \quad 0 \leq x \leq 1, \; 0 \leq y \leq 1$$

Find $P(X < Y)$ — the probability that Alice arrives before Bob.
