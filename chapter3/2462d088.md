### [Hard] Maximum and Expected Value of Independent Random Variables

**Problem:**
A company dispatches 3 autonomous delivery robots simultaneously for an important event. The time it takes for each robot to reach the destination, $X_1, X_2$, and $X_3$ (in minutes), are perfectly independent of each other, and all follow a continuous uniform distribution between 0 and 10 minutes. 

The event can only start when **all 3 robots have arrived**. Let the random variable $Y$ be the final waiting time until the event starts. What is the expected waiting time, $E[Y]$, in minutes?
