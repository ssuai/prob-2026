2. Normal

Consider two continuous random variables, X and Y, with a joint PDF f_{X,Y}(x,y) that holds a constant value c over the region 0<=x<=2 and 0<=y<=1, and is 0 elsewhere. First, determine the value of the constant c by applying the normalization property ($\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_{X,Y}(x,y) \,dx\,dy = 1). Then, verify whether the relationship f_{X,Y}(x,y) = f_X(x)f_Y(y) holds for all x and y to determine the independence of the two variables.
