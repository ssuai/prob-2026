## Context: Data Center Reliability (Exponential Distribution & CDF)
A tech company manages a server farm that occasionally experiences power grid failures. The time $T$ (in months) until the next failure follows an exponential distribution with an unknown rate parameter $\lambda$. A new uninterruptible power supply (UPS) is installed to mitigate these risks. For risk management compliance, the company wants to ensure that the probability of a power failure occurring within the first quarter (3 months) is strictly less than a specific threshold probability $p$ (where $0 < p < 1$).

## Problem
Derive the mathematical condition on the expected time between failures, $E[T]$, that satisfies this strict compliance requirement. Express your final answer entirely in terms of $p$.
