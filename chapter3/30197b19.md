[Hard] 3. Normal Distribution & Standardization

Problem:The scores of a statistics exam follow a normal distribution with a mean of 70 and a standard deviation of 10 ($X \sim \mathcal{N}(70, 10^2)$).

1) Find the probability that a student scores between 65 and 85.
2) If the top 5% of students receive an 'A' grade, find the minimum score required for an 'A'. (Assume $\Phi(1.645) = 0.95$ and $\Phi(1) = 0.8413$)
