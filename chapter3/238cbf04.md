3. Hard: The Dependent Region
Let $X$ and $Y$ be two continuous random variables with a joint probability density function $f(x, y)$ defined over a triangular region:$$f(x, y) =\begin{cases}8xy & 0 \le y \le x \le 1 \0 & \text{otherwise}\end{cases}$$
Task A: Find the marginal probability density functions $f_X(x)$ and $f_Y(y)$. Be careful with the limits of integration.
Task B: Are $X$ and $Y$ independent? Provide a rigorous mathematical proof for your conclusion.
Task C: Determine the conditional density function $f_{X|Y}(x|y)$.
Task D: Calculate the conditional expectation $E[X | Y = y]$. Express your answer as a function of $y$.
