## Problem

Let the joint probability density function of $(X,Y)$ be given by

$$
f_{X,Y}(x,y) =
\begin{cases}
c(x + y), & 0 \le x \le 1,\; 0 \le y \le 1 \\
0, & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
$$

1. Find the constant $c$.
2. Find the marginal density $f_X(x)$.
3. Compute $P(X \le 0.5)$.
