# Problem 2

**Difficulty:** Intermediate

Continuous random variables \(X\) and \(Y\) have joint pdf

\[
f_{X,Y}(x,y)=
\begin{cases}
c, & 0\le y\le x\le 2,\\
0, & \text{otherwise}.
\end{cases}
\]

1. Find the constant \(c\).
2. Find the marginal pdf \(f_X(x)\).

**Main Topic:** Joint PDF, marginal PDF
