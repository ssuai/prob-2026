# Hard Problem — Circular Uniform PDF

## Problem

A dart is thrown at a circular target of radius $r$, centered at the origin. All points of impact $(x, y)$ are equally likely, so:

$$f_{X,Y}(x,y) = \frac{1}{\pi r^2}, \quad x^2 + y^2 \leq r^2$$

**True or False:** The marginal PDF $f_Y(y)$ is uniform. Justify your answer.

---

## Answer

**FALSE.**

$$f_Y(y) = \frac{2}{\pi r^2}\sqrt{r^2 - y^2}, \quad -r \leq y \leq r$$

This depends on $y$, so it is **not** uniform.
