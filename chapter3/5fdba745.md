**Question 4: 2D Uniform Distribution and Transformations**
A point $(X,Y)$ is chosen uniformly at random from the circular disk $x^2 + y^2 \leq 4$.
- **(a)** Write down the joint PDF $f_{X,Y}(x,y)$.
- **(b)** Determine if $X$ and $Y$ are independent. Justify your answer using marginal PDFs.
- **(c)** Let $R = \sqrt{X^2 + Y^2}$ be the distance from the origin. Find the CDF of $R$, $F_R(r)$, and its expected value $E[R]$.
