Problem 3: Hard (Focus: Joint PDF, Marginalization, & Independence)
Let two continuous random variables $X$ and $Y$ have the following joint PDF:$$f_{X,Y}(x,y) = \begin{cases} x + y & \text{for } 0 \le x \le 1 \text{ and } 0 \le y \le 1 \\ 0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$
1. Marginalization: Find the marginal PDF of $X$, $f_X(x)$.
2. Conditioning: Find the conditional PDF of $Y$ given $X$, $f_{Y|X}(y|x)$.
3. Independence: Are $X$ and $Y$ independent? Prove your answer using the definition of independence from your summary.
