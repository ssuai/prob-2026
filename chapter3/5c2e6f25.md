# 3. Joint PDF of Continuous Random Variables (Hard)

A rescue drone is monitoring a triangular search zone. Let \(X\) and \(Y\) denote the horizontal and vertical coordinates, respectively, of a signal detected by the drone. Assume that the signal is equally likely to originate from any point inside the triangular region

\[
S=\{(x,y)\mid 0\le x\le 2,\ 0\le y\le x\}.
\]

The joint probability density function of \(X\) and \(Y\) is given by

\[
f_{X,Y}(x,y)=
\begin{cases}
c, & (x,y)\in S,\\
0, & \text{otherwise}.
\end{cases}
\]

Answer the following questions.

(a) Find the constant \(c\) by using the normalization condition for a joint PDF.

(b) Find the marginal PDF \(f_X(x)\).

(c) Find the marginal PDF \(f_Y(y)\).

(d) Find the joint cumulative distribution function

\[
F_{X,Y}(x,y)=P(X\le x,\;Y\le y)
\]

for \(0\le x\le 2\) and \(0\le y\le 2\). Your answer should be given piecewise, depending on the relationship between \(x\) and \(y\).

(e) Find the conditional PDF

\[
f_{Y|X}(y|x)
\]

for values of \(x\) such that \(f_X(x)>0\).
