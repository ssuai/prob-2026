# Hard Problem (Joint Distribution and Normal Distribution)

Let X and Y be independent normal random variables such that:

X ~ N(2, 1)  
Y ~ N(3, 4)

1. Find the mean and variance of Z = X + Y.
2. Find P(Z > 6).
3. Standardize Z and express the probability using the standard normal variable.
4. Find P(X < 1 and Y > 2).
